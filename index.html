Frequently Asked Questions

Am I able to book urgent on the day appointments?

Yes, you can use our Ask NHS app (downloadble from Google Playstore or App store) to book urgent and routine appointments.

How do I request medication online?

You can request your repeat medication online via Patient Access app (Please email us to gain login details) or Ask NHS app.

What type of appointments do you have?

We offer a range of different appointments such as urgent on the day, routine telephone calls, video consulations and all kind of nurses appointments.

Can only students register?

This website is for students, if you are not a student, please register at premiermedicalcentre.nhs.uk
