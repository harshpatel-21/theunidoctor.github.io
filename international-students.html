WELCOME! WE WOULD LIKE TO GIVE YOU SOME TIPS

What’s this about?

We have been collaborating for years with MDX University London, looking after the health of the students.
In this particular year, we think it is important to provide you with useful links and tips about the health system in the UK and what’s important for you.
We want to be proactive and support your journey!

FIRST OF ALL:

Register with a Doctor!!!
It’s free, fast and you need to do it in order to obtain your NHS (National Health Service) Number that you’ll need for any health related issue.
REGISTER HERE!

WHAT ABOUT COVID-19?

Before you travel… check the UK Gov website:
1. CHECK THE UK ENTRY REQUIREMENTS (UK Gov site)
2. BOOK YOUR ENTRY COVID TESTS (Test provider site, discounted rates for Patients from MDX Uni)
Once you have arrived…
If you have symptoms, book a FREE Covid test (remember you will need to be registered first to have your NHS Number)
• Book your NHS PCR Covid Test if symptomatic HERE

AND THE VACCINE?

If you have had it outside the UK, contact your GP and let them know to update your records
If you still don’t have it we recommend you to get it ASAP. You can book it HERE
